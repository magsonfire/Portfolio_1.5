Avcon WW: Aviation Consulting

Agency: Chartreuse Media
Project: WordPress Website, Graphic & Brochure for Avcon WW

Summary

AVCON.WW inc. is a solutions provider to airports, airlines and the aviation-related community.

This project involved customizing the stylesheets, PHP injection, and interactive elements of a WordPress blog to match AVCON.WW inc.'s branding and direct users' attention towards the business's specialized services. The goal was to have users look at the homepage and instantly realize the company's consulting specialties and their environmental sustainability focus.
