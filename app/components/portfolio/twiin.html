Portfolio: Twiin

Twiin: Lovers Rock Singer

Agency: Chartreuse Media
Project: Integrated Marketing for Twiin Jesse

Summary

Twiin Jesse is a lovers rock singer/swongwriter, whose tunes borrow from the streetlight magic of night-time Toronto. His sound is a little bit country, a little bit soul, and entirely homegrown.

This project involved developing a one-of-a-kind responsive one-page website that showcases Twiin's unique look, and invites visitors to download his press kit (*hint* go towards the light!).
