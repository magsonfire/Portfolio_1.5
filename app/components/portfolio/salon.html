Salon Series

Agency: Chartreuse Media
Project: Semi-Regular Salon for Local Artists and Intellectuals

Summary

The Salon Series is a bimonthly community event hosted by Chartreuse Media, a local integrated marketing agency, to bring together artists and intellectuals in Toronto. Each salon is themed around a special activity or event.

For this project, I conceived, planned, and designed the digital and print marketing material for the 'Alice In Wonderland'-themed salon, entiteld "Down the Rabbit Hole".
