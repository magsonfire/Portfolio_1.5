Welcome to Your Tutorial

Tutorials are the small-group learning experience that complements the lectures. Here you will discuss the lectures, engage in illustrative activities, and receive guidance on your essays.

Tutorials are compulsory!

Also note that tuorials do not take place not every week. See below for information about tutorial sign up, schedules, and participation evaluation.

How do I sign up?

The sign up for tutorials is on a first-come-first-served basis. Note that there is a deadline, provided on Blackboard. You will also be signing up via Blackboard.

Blackboard refers to tutorials as "Groups". Select the group/tutorial you would like to jion by clicking on the radio button next to the name of the group/tutorial you would like to join. (Example: A group named "T1-2" means a tutorial that occurs on Tuesdays from 1-2pm.)

Attention: There may be more than one page of tutorials available! Be sure to check all pages.

After the deadline for signing up, if you do not have a tutorial we will assign you one, and you may not be able to choose the date and time.

Where and when are my tutorials?

All tutorials are held in the Anthropology Building, room AP 302. This room is dedicated to Anthropology 100.

Everyone's tutorial is on a different day and on different weeks. Follow the tutorial schedule to see when yours is. (But first make sure you've signed up for a tutorial day and time.)

How do I contact my Teaching Assistant?

Check the TA List below to see what your TA looks like, as well as their contact information.

I forgot which tutorial I’m in!

To check which tutorial you are in, you need to go to Blackboard and click on "Course Tools" on the left hand menu, then select "UT Group Memberships". This will show you the tutorial that you are in (you may have to click on a few more things - it should be intuitive enough.)
