Read Before Lecture & Check Back Often!

Make sure that you do these readings before the lecture each week.

Also come back to this page often - it may be updated without notice, so it's not a good idea to just print it and never check here again.

About Lecture Notes & Study Questions

In addition, each lecture topic will also contain a link to the class notes provided by the instructor. These sum up some of the lecture's main points. These questions are very likely to be asked on the test or exam in one form or another (not necessarily identical to how they appear in the notes), or at any rate cover topics that you are very likely to be tested on. The lecture notes and questions, though, are by no means a substitute for taking your own notes, and certainly not for the lecture itself. The slides used in class will not be posted online.

Lecture notes and questions will be available after each lecture.

You can also click below for a list of all study notes and questions (although the content itself will not be available until the lecture has passed).
